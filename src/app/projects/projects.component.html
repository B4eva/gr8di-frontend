Docs Translation

✻

One of the struggles that many new groovy developers face is finding documentation in their native language. The project to translate the Groovy docs into other languages (at first, French) will help many new developers get the assistance they need to become the skilled developers they want to be.

View translated docs and learn more

Student Ambassador Program

✻

One way to help new people find Groovy is at the University level. The GR8DI student ambassador program, encourages students to foster for a more diversity groovy community by advocating for Groovy technologies at their university, in side projects, at their students jobs, and to teach or organize events to promote Apache groovy in their local communities.

Mentorship Program

✻

In conjunction with the Groovy school program, we are seeking volunteers to help mentor the students into becoming successful Groovy experts. Mentors can expect to answer basic questions, recommend tutorials, blog posts, or books to help the student progress. They may also assist or code review side projects or business ideas for the students. To volunteer, fill out the google form here. To sign up as a student in the program, fill out the form here.
